Instruction Sets for Strangers

2019/08/31

World Trade Center Transportation Hub

AEIOU Design Method

We researched and recorded the activities, environment, interaction, objects and users in the World Trade Center Transportation Hub.


Since most of the people were just walking through the square, we decided to focus on those poeple who used the cloud shape benches in the World Trade Center.



Behavioral Mapping

We recorded the routes and events of 10 users of those benches.



Brainstorming Tree



Concept Sketch


The cloud is made of cotton and it has a speaker, lights, and sensors inside. If nobody sits on the cloud, it will be the same white color as the other cloud shape benches in the center. However, if there’s anyone sits on it, it will produce sound and turn into yellow. If there’s another person sit on it, the cloud will be divided into 2 parts with different colors of light. With the numbers of people sit on the cloud increase, the cloud will be colorful and produce more sound. Since this cloud is playful with multiplayer, we’d like to encourage people to invite not only their friends but also strangers to sit on this cloud with them to create a unique cloud with colors. It is a good chance for people to talk with strangers and take photos to record this moment.



Test Question

👉 If blinking lights could attract pedestrians?

👉 Are pedestrians willing to sit on a special cloud?

👉 Is there any interaction between strangers?



Prototype & On-site Test

First version

People are unwilling to sit down, maybe the fabric looks weird, and not very clean to sit.

Second version

When the cloud is empty, the lights are blinking to attract pedestrians.

When someone sits down, the lights are keeping on.

People were very interested and would like to see the effect of sitting down, maybe because this cloud looks similar as other clouds, but this one is very different and special.

Test Video

We are glad to see that many adults and kids were attracted by the blinking cloud and were willing to use it!

Feedback & Problems

Update 9/17

Instruction Sets Final

Inspiration

Dove is a symbolic of peace. The designer of the station, Santiago Calatrava was inspired by a picture of a child releasing a dove for flying. That’s why we use dove as an element in our installation.

The Ghost Festival is a traditional Buddhist festival held in certain East Asian countries. On that day, people release paper boats and lanterns on water to give directions to the lost ghosts and spirits of the ancestors.


Brainstorming

911是美国人心中永远的伤疤，他也永远在提醒人们和平是多么重要。我们想用在水池里放lantern of dove的方式来纪念遇难者，寄托我们的哀思并为他们指明来生的方向，并希望我们的世界永远和平。


Sketch

We planned to use a cloud shape pool to fit the environment in the Oculus, pour water in it. We also made a lot of transparent boxes with doves and lights in them, and put a few of them in the water and put the left near the pool, which would imply others put the boxes in the water.
