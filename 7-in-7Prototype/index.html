Prototype 2: Spiral Music

Question

If music could be used to enhance children's ability of memory?


Concept

Inspired by the Simon Says game, I made this prototype. In my mind, Music is just like memory, it's not flat, it's three-dimensional. This device has a spiral-shape keyboard with a glass cover, every octave is made to be a circle, the tones would play in an spcecific order with the light of the key flashing, so that the music would be played in a three-dimensional form, which gives people more visualized experience. The outside surface of the keys are touchable, so that users are able to play music by themselves. I'm thinking to use this device to educate children musicality and enhance their ability of memory. I'm also thinking that parents could even transmit a song from their cellphones, and the device would play that song.


Development

I built the model of the device, and designed 2 versions of the shape.
