Instruction Sets for Strangers

2019/08/31

World Trade Center Transportation Hub

AEIOU Design Method

We researched and recorded the activities, environment, interaction, objects and users in the World Trade Center Transportation Hub.


Since most of the people were just walking through the square, we decided to focus on those poeple who used the cloud shape benches in the World Trade Center.


Behavioral Mapping

We recorded the routes and events of 10 users of those benches.


Brainstorming Tree
