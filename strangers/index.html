Instruction Sets for Strangers

2019/08/31

World Trade Center Transportation Hub

AEIOU Design Method

We researched and recorded the activities, environment, interaction, objects and users in the World Trade Center Transportation Hub.


Since most of the people were just walking through the square, we decided to focus on those poeple who used the cloud shape benches in the World Trade Center.



Behavioral Mapping

We recorded the routes and events of 10 users of those benches.



Brainstorming Tree



Concept Sketch


The cloud is made of cotton and it has a speaker, lights, and sensors inside. If nobody sits on the cloud, it will be the same white color as the other cloud shape benches in the center. However, if thereâ€™s anyone sits on it, it will produce sound and turn into yellow. If thereâ€™s another person sit on it, the cloud will be divided into 2 parts with different colors of light. With the numbers of people sit on the cloud increase, the cloud will be colorful and produce more sound. Since this cloud is playful with multiplayer, weâ€™d like to encourage people to invite not only their friends but also strangers to sit on this cloud with them to create a unique cloud with colors. It is a good chance for people to talk with strangers and take photos to record this moment.

Prototype & On-site Test

First version

People are unwilling to sit down, maybe the fabric looks weird, and not very clean to sit.

Second version

When the cloud is empty, the lights are blinking to attract pedestrians.

When someone sits down, the lights are keeping on.

People were very interested and would like to see the effect of sitting down, maybe because this cloud looks similar as other clouds, but this one is very different and special.

Test Video

We are glad to see that many adults and kids were attracted by the blinking cloud and were willing to use it!
