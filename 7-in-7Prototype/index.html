Prototype 1: If Photos Sing

Question

How can photos play music?


Concept

According to my research, I found most people like to take photos to keep their memory, so I'm thinking if the photos they take could play a music which the user listened to at that time, it would become a time machine to bring the user back to a moment in his or her past life.

Users need to store a peice of music in the photo when they print it. When they put the photo which was printed on a kind of special paper on the device, it plays the piece of music automatically. Users are able to adjust the volume with the touch bar of side.

Prototype 2: Spiral Music

Question

If music could be used to enhance children's ability of memory?


Concept

Inspired by the Simon Says game, I made this prototype. In my mind, Music is just like memory, it's not flat, it's three-dimensional. This device has a spiral-shape keyboard with a glass cover, each octave is a round, the tones would play in a spcecific order with the light of the key flashing, so that the music would be played in a three-dimensional form, which gives people more visualized experience.

The outside surface of the keys are touchable, so that users are able to play music by themselves. I'm thinking to use this device to educate children musicality and enhance their ability of memory. I'm also thinking that parents could even transmit a song from their cellphones, and the device would play that song.


Development

I built the model of the device, and designed 2 versions of the shape.

Prototype 3: Musical Memory of City

Question

How could a music map show the memory of a city?


Concept

At the end of last century, with the rapid development of entertainment industry in Hong Kong, many classic pop songs emerged, many of which were named after the streets and landmarks of Hong Kong. It can be said that Hong Kong music carries the city memories of a generation of Hong Kong people.


Development

I designed a website or maybe an application to show the music map of Hongkong.

Outcome

As you can see on the map, I marked the name of the places which relate to a specific song, when you click on the mark, the song played. I'm also thinking to add more cities expcept Hong Kong.
